Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries about reaching us, our consultation process, and what to expect when you contact DH Construction.

Still Have Questions? Call Us
Is the consultation really free?

Yes, absolutely. We offer a free site visit and consultation for all renovation enquiries. Our team will visit your property, understand your requirements, and provide a detailed written estimate — all at no cost and no obligation.

How quickly will I receive a response?

We respond to all enquiries within 24 hours. For urgent requests, calling or WhatsApp-ing us gets you an instant response. We'll schedule a site visit at your convenience within 1–2 business days.

Do I need to prepare anything before the site visit?

Not necessarily, but it helps to have a rough idea of what you want changed, any reference photos (from Pinterest or Instagram), and your budget range. If you have existing floor plans, keep those handy too.

Can I get a quote over the phone without a site visit?

We can give you a rough ballpark range over the phone based on your description. However, an accurate and binding estimate requires a site visit to assess the current condition, measurements, and specific requirements.

What if I'm located outside Chennai?

While our primary service area is Chennai and its suburbs, we do take on projects in nearby areas like Kanchipuram, Chengalpattu, and Tiruvallur. Contact us with your location and we'll let you know if we can help.

Can I visit your office without an appointment?

Yes, you're welcome to walk in during our office hours (Mon–Sat, 8 AM – 7 PM). However, for a detailed discussion about your project, we recommend scheduling an appointment so we can dedicate proper time to your requirements.
