Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about your renovation project? Here are answers to the most common ones we hear from our clients.

Still Have Questions?
How much does a home renovation cost in Chennai?

It depends on the scope and materials chosen. A basic bathroom renovation starts from ₹60,000, a kitchen from ₹1.5 lakhs, and full home renovation from ₹8–10 lakhs. We provide a detailed, itemized quote after a free site visit.

How long does a typical renovation take?

Bathroom renovations take 10–15 days, kitchen remodeling 15–25 days, and complete home renovation 30–60 days depending on the area and complexity. We commit to a timeline upfront and stick to it.

Do I need to vacate my home during renovation?

Not always. For single-room renovations (kitchen or bathroom), you can stay. For full home renovation involving electrical and plumbing overhaul, we recommend temporary relocation for safety and convenience.

Do you handle permits and approvals?

Yes, for renovations involving structural changes, we guide you through the necessary approvals from the local municipality or panchayat. For interior-only work, permits are typically not required.

What materials do you use? Can I choose my own?

We use premium-grade materials from trusted brands. You're absolutely welcome to choose your own materials, tiles, fixtures, and finishes — we'll source them at the best prices and handle all procurement.

Do you provide a warranty on renovation work?

Yes, we provide a workmanship warranty of 1–2 years depending on the type of work. Waterproofing carries a separate manufacturer warranty. We also offer post-handover support for any issues.
