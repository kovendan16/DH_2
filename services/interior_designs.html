FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our interior design and fit-out services? Here are answers to the most common ones.

Still Have Questions?
How long does a typical office fit-out take?
For a standard office of 2,000–5,000 sq ft, the fit-out typically takes 25–45 days depending on the complexity. Larger spaces or custom designs may take 45–60 days. We provide a detailed timeline before starting work.
Do you provide 3D designs before starting work?
Yes! For all premium and luxury packages, we provide detailed 3D visualizations so you can see exactly how your space will look before any work begins. Essential packages include basic layout plans.
What is the minimum area you work with?
We handle projects starting from 500 sq ft upwards. There's no maximum limit — we've done everything from small cabins to entire corporate floors of 20,000+ sq ft.
Can I use my existing furniture in the new design?
Absolutely. We integrate your existing furniture into the new design wherever possible. Our designers will assess what can be reused and suggest complementary pieces for a cohesive look.
Do you offer warranties on the work?
Yes, we provide a 1-year warranty on all fit-out work including false ceilings, partitions, and furniture. Electrical work comes with a 2-year warranty. Extended warranty options are also available.
Can work be done on weekends or after hours?
Yes, for operational offices and commercial spaces, we can schedule work during weekends or after business hours to minimize disruption. This may have a small scheduling surcharge.
