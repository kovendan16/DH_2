FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our office infrastructure and fit-out services.

Still Have Questions?
What is the typical timeline for an office fit-out?
For a standard office of 2,000–5,000 sq ft, we complete the fit-out in 25–35 days. Larger spaces of 10,000+ sq ft typically take 45–60 days. We also offer fast-track options with parallel work streams to reduce timelines.
Do you work during weekends or after office hours?
Yes, this is one of our key advantages. We can schedule work during weekends, nights, or holidays to ensure zero disruption to your ongoing business operations. A small scheduling surcharge may apply.
What's included in a turnkey office fit-out?
Our turnkey package includes partitions (glass/gypsum), false ceiling, flooring, electrical & lighting, data cabling, painting, furniture installation, glass doors, branding wall, and professional cleanup — everything needed for a move-in ready office.
Can you work with our existing office layout?
Absolutely. We can reconfigure your existing layout, add new partitions, upgrade ceilings, or refresh flooring and painting. Partial fit-out and renovation services are available for offices that need upgrades without a full rebuild.
Do you provide 3D designs before starting?
Yes, for all projects above 3,000 sq ft, we provide detailed 3D visualizations of the planned workspace. For smaller projects, we provide 2D layout plans with material samples and finish references.
What warranty do you offer?
We provide a 2-year warranty on all civil and fit-out work including partitions, ceilings, and flooring. Electrical work carries a 2-year warranty. Furniture warranties are as per manufacturer terms. Post-warranty maintenance is available at competitive rates.
